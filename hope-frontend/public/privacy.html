Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hope Universe collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Telegram Mini App. Our approach is simple: collect only what we need to run the product fairly and securely.

1. Information We Collect

  • Telegram account identifiers and basic profile fields shared by Telegram (for authentication and account continuity).
  • App activity data, such as tasks, streaks, points, tickets, game progress, and referral events.
  • Wallet address and transaction proof data when you connect a wallet or use payment-based features.
  • Technical and security logs (device/session metadata, request logs, anti-abuse signals).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users and keep your app state in sync across pages and sessions.
  • Calculate rewards, rankings, referrals, and eligibility for features like contests and quests.
  • Verify payments and prevent fraud, abuse, duplicate actions, and unfair manipulation.
  • Maintain service reliability, troubleshoot incidents, and improve product performance.

3. Wallet and Transaction Privacy

We store your wallet address and transaction verification fields needed for feature fulfillment and auditability. Blockchain networks are public by design; on-chain data can be visible to anyone.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data only when necessary with:

  • Infrastructure/service providers that help us run the app securely.
  • Compliance or law-enforcement authorities when required by law.
  • Fraud-prevention workflows needed to protect the community and ecosystem integrity.

5. Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the service, protect against abuse, resolve disputes, and satisfy legal or operational obligations.

6. Security

We apply technical and organizational safeguards to reduce risk. No system is completely risk-free, so you should also protect your Telegram account and wallet access credentials.

7. Your Choices

  • You can disconnect your wallet at any time.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can request account/privacy support through our official Telegram support channel.

8. Children

The app is not intended for users who are legally considered children in their jurisdiction without required guardian consent.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as features evolve. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised version.

10. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact the Hope Universe team via our official Telegram support channel.
